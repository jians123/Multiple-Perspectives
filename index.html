The Imperial Palace

The Forbidden City in Beijing, formerly known as the Forbidden City, is the imperial palace of the Ming and Qing dynasties in China. It is located at the center of the central axis of Beijing. Centered on the three halls, the Forbidden City of Beijing covers an area of about 720000 square meters, with a building area of about 150000 square meters. It has more than 70 palaces and more than 9000 houses.
